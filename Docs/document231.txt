More featured recipes » Barbecued Spare Ribs are a classic American barbecue meal, made from pork. This version is cooked in the oven, not on a barbecue, and uses a barbecue sauce with a distinct Asian flavor, featuring soy sauce, rice vinegar, and garlic. Image credit More featured ingredients » Cilantro is the leafy part of the coriander plant. It has a very different, much more fresh, taste than coriander seed. Cilantro is an important ingredient in Asian cuisine and in Mexican Salsas. It is typically purchased fresh, in a bundle, in a manner similar to fresh parsley.
